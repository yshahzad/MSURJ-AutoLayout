About the MSURJ AutoLayout Tool

This tool was originally created as a "passion project" by Yavuz, an MSURJ senior editor and U3 Statistics major. His research interests lie mainly in biostatistics and machine learning in healthcare, and he has performed research with the BRAIN and D3Mod labs. Two of his other passion projects include InsideRadar, a platform meant to detect insider trading, and ProfSight McGill, an interactive dashboard to help McGill undergrads make informed decisions during degree planning. Somehow, amidst his research, personal projects, and being in his final year of studies, he found time to make this tool for MSURJ editors to help us out with layout ... Thank you so much Yavuz!

To read more about Yavuz, take a look at his personal website.
